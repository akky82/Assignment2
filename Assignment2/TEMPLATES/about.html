About Us

Django Weather Services was established in October 2021 in Brisbane, Australia.

With a passion for golf, day-trips, hiking and other travel, we found ourselves checking the weather constantly, and sometimes looking for the forecast/conditions for a campsite far from urban areas wasn't always easily available. So, our team decided to create our own solution and bring it to life for the world to use.

We are expanding our site in the future and look forward to sharing our innovations with our users by providing access to helpful tools that we want to use too.

D. Hill - Managing Director
